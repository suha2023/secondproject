ABOUT ITS MAKING..

Best muffin Cake

Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl. Add the egg, milk, and butter, stirring only enough to dampen the dry ingredients; the batter should not be smooth. Spoon into the muffin cups, filling each one about two-thirds full. Bake for about 20 to 25 minutes each

Croissont blueberrie

Mix together the cream cheese, lemon zest and sugar, divide between the croissants placing at the small side end. Top with the blueberries and roll to form crescents. Place on a lightly greased baking tray approximately 5 cm apart. Bake for 10-15 mins until risen and golden brown.

pain suisse

Mix together the cream cheese, and sugar, divide between the croissants placing at the small side end. Top with the chocolate and roll to form crescents. Place on a lightly greased baking tray approximately 5 cm apart. Bake for 10-15 mins
